We're glad to announce that we have obtained funding from [SLU Grogrund](https://www.slu.se/en/about-slu/organisation/collaborative-centres/slu-grogrund/) for the project _Breeder co-pilot - Optimizing plant breeding with AI_. Aleksis from the RIDR group
will lead an AI-centric work package, as well as be co-supervisor for an industrial PhD student at Lantmännen (main supervisor at SLU).
